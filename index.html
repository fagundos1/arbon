About TherapyLogy

TherapyLogy was born from a simple belief: mental health support should be accessible, affordable, and available to everyone. We understand that seeking help can be challenging, which is why we've created a platform that removes barriers and makes mental health care approachable.

Our team of mental health professionals, technologists, and designers work together to create an experience that truly supports your well-being. We're committed to evidence-based approaches and continuously improving our platform based on user feedback and research.
